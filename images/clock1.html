      Welcome to Clarks Inn Suites, Near Airport, Raipur, Chhattisgarh

      Raipur is a modern city with incredible industrial growth, yet its roots go back to the Mauryan Empire. The capital city of Chhattisgarh, Raipur attracts huge footfall year-round from business and leisure guests.
       











       

      Clarks Inn Suites, Raipur is a 4-star hotel providing a fabulous stay with best-in-class services. It stands as a testament to world-class hospitality with its courteous staff, luxurious stay and delicious food. Located centrally, our hotel in Raipur provides easy access to places like City Mall 36, Magneto Mall, Marine Drive and Nandan Van Zoo. It also boasts facilities like 4 event venues, a restaurant, a fitness centre, and a spa, that are designed to serve you well. Welcome to Clarks Inn Suites, Raipur!
       

      A Ritzy Stay

       
      To serve you during your stay in Raipur, we have three room options. The Executive Rooms are our standard offerings that come with a balcony and a king-sized bed. The Premium Rooms come with the same bells and whistles with much more space. The Suites are ostentatious accommodations spread across 528 sq. ft., with a separate living room. Have a delightful repose at our 4 star hotel in Raipur!
