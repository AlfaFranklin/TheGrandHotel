Savour Local Flavours. It’ll blow your mind.

Go on a flavour-filled ride at kimiya, our multi-cuisine restaurant. Have a brilliant breakfast buffet, choose from a curated list of delicacies like pahadi dhaniya kukad, Asian slice Paneer, Mutton Rogan Josh, Tandoori and Biryani for lunch and have a relaxed dining experiance in the evenings. We also have a modern pure-vegetarian fine-dining restaurant called UNS which opens in the evening. Have a delightful dining experience at the Grand Hotel.

Embrace Wellness the Grand Way. See For Yourself

Are you sore after an exciting sightseeing trip or a successful business meeting? Get rejuvenated at Divine, our in-house spa, with massage treatments like aromatherapy, Swedish massage, deep tissue therapy, and Thai massage among others.

Embark on a transformative wellness voyage. Checkmate.

Experience a premier fitness facility right at your doorstep with our state-of-the-art hotel gym. Elevate your stay by indulging in a comprehensive wellness journey tailored to your needs. Our well-equipped gym offers a diverse range of modern exercise equipment, ensuring you have all you need for a rewarding workout.

Celebrate and Socialise See For Yourself

To facilitate your celebrations and other social events, we have four event halls. Classique is a spacious hall for social events, while Kohinoor serves as a conference space very well. Grassia is our lawn that is spread across 10800 sq. ft., where glorious weddings can be conducted. We also have a board room for your official event needs. All of these venues come with amenities such as Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, a projector and audio-visual equipment for the smooth functioning of your events.
